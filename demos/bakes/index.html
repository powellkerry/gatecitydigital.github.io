Our Story

A little bakery with a lot of heart

Powell Bakes started with a passion for bringing people together through beautiful, delicious baked goods. Based right here in Pocatello, Idaho, every cake and cupcake is made from scratch in small batches — never mass-produced.

Whether you're planning a wedding, celebrating a birthday, or just want something sweet for any occasion, we work closely with you to bring your vision to life. No two cakes are exactly alike, and that's exactly the point.

All orders are made fresh to order. We recommend reaching out at least 2–3 weeks in advance for custom cakes, and 1 week for cupcake orders.

How It Works

From vision to your table

Ordering a custom cake is easy and fun. Here's what to expect:

1

Get in Touch

Fill out our order form or DM us on Instagram. Tell us your date, event type, and any ideas you have.

2

Design Together

We'll chat through flavors, colors, and design inspiration to create something perfectly you.

3

Confirm & Deposit

Once the design is approved, a 50% deposit secures your date. Balance due at pickup.

4

Pickup or Delivery

Your cake is ready fresh on your day. Pickup in Pocatello or local delivery available.
