Our Mission

Dedicated to promoting equality, diversity, and acceptance for every LGBTQ+ individual and their allies in Southeastern Idaho.

Sage & Rainbow LGBTQ+ Advocates of Southeastern Idaho is a nonprofit organization committed to providing a safe, welcoming, and affirming environment for LGBTQ+ individuals, their families, and allies throughout the Pocatello region and beyond.

What We Believe

We believe that every person deserves to live authentically, free from discrimination and fear. We believe in the power of community — that when we come together, we are stronger, more visible, and more capable of creating lasting change.

What We Do

  • Provide free HIV testing and sexual health resources
  • Connect community members with local and national support services
  • Offer a safe space for LGBTQ+ youth and adults
  • Advocate for LGBTQ+ rights at the local, state, and national level
  • Support school Gender & Sexuality Alliances (GSAs)
  • Raise awareness through education and outreach

In the News

Featured in HIV Plus Magazine as a groundbreaking center offering free HIV testing and making a real difference for young people in Pocatello, Idaho. The Advocate also profiled our work rescuing LGBTQ+ youth in rural Idaho.

The Moment We're In

In past years, our LGBTQ+ community has achieved great advances toward true equality. We remain committed to protecting those gains and fighting for full equality for every member of our community — through education, advocacy, and love.
