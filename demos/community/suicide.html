You Matter

LGBTQ+ individuals face significantly higher rates of depression, anxiety, and suicidal ideation — often as a result of stigma, discrimination, family rejection, and isolation. These feelings are real, valid, and treatable.

Being LGBTQ+ is not the problem. The problem is a world that hasn't yet fully accepted you. Your identity is valid, beautiful, and worthy of love. Please reach out. Communities like ours exist because you matter.
