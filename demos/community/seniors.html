LGBT Seniors

Resources, community, and advocacy for LGBTQ+ elders in Southeastern Idaho.

LGBTQ+ older adults face unique challenges — discrimination in senior care facilities, social isolation, and health disparities. We are committed to ensuring that LGBTQ+ seniors are treated with dignity and respect.
