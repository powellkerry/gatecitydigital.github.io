Get Involved

Join us in building a more inclusive, affirming community for LGBTQ+ people in Southeastern Idaho.

Volunteers are the heart of Sage & Rainbow. Whether you have an hour a month or several hours a week, your time and energy make a real difference in the lives of LGBTQ+ individuals across our community.

Ways to Volunteer

  • Help with outreach events and community tabling
  • Support HIV testing and health education programs
  • Mentor LGBTQ+ youth
  • Help with social media and communications
  • Assist with fundraising and grant writing
  • Share your professional skills (legal, medical, design, tech, etc.)
